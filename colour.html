Colour

Jade

The colour scheme is based on jade. The origin of CBD in a medical context lays in China, where they also associated jade with purity and inner beauty. This fits in perfectly with the brand values.

Primary colour

Chemistry Green is the main colour throughout the entire branding and is used for headings, subheadings, icons, ...

Chemistry Green
Pantone: Pantone 3435C
Hex: #004631
RGB: 0, 70, 49
CMYK: 100, 40, 87, 53

Secondary colours

Jade green is used to create small accents and Chemistry Gray is used for (almost) all body text.
