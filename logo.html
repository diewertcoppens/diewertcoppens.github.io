Colour variations

The logo can be used in several colour variations.

Positive

This is the primary logo, use this version as much as possible to guarantee consistancy.

Negative

In case the positive logo can’t be used, the negative version can be an alternative. However, it’s advised to use the positive version as much as possible.

Gold foil

The logo can also be embossed in gold coloured foil on a jade or white background.

Monochrome

In case colour printing is not available, a monochrome alternative can be used.

Logo misuse

The logo is a big part of the brand identity, that's why it's important to always display it correctly.

Do not invert the logo.

Respect the colour rules.

Do not tilt the logo.

Do not use the logo outlined.

Do not stretch the logo in any direction.

Do not fill the logo.

Do not add an outline.

Do not add a dropshadow.

Do not resolve the logo in different colours.

Do not scale the elements individually.

Do not change the font.

Respect the logo lockups.
