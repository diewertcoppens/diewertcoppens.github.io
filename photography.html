Photography

The (im)proper use of photography can make or break your design. That's why it's important to use correct images.

The following are several rules to ensure the look and feel of the photos is consistent

All images should have the same look and feel to them

All images should fit within the brand colour scheme

All images should portray a calm atmosphere

Do not use illustrations, duotone or black and white images
