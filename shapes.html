Shapes

There are three recurring shapes throughout the entire branding:

Seal shape

The purpose of integrating a seal shape into some designs is to visually translate a feeling of professionalism and safety. The packaging, for example, has this element to imply that the product is securely packaged.

Chinese inspired border

CBD originally found its roots in China. As a small reference to this history, this Chinese inspired border is used throughout the branding, especially as an embossed detail around the borders of the packaging.

Oil stain

This oil stain shape can be used as a decorative element to add a dynamic touch to the designs.
