Textures

There are three recurring shapes throughout the entire branding:

Jade stone

The use of jade throughout the branding is a reference to the history of CBD. Back when CBD started being used in a medical context in China thousands of years ago, the Chinese regarded jade as the most precious stone; it symbolised purity and inner beauty.

Gold

Ancient Chemistry is all about the health and well-being of the customer, they should feel like they're put in first place. For this reason, all packaging should convey a sense of luxury. It makes the user feel like they're taken care of and that they are given the best there is. Adding gold embellishments is a way to visually convey this feeling.
