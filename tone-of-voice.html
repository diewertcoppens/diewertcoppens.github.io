Tone of voice

Ancient Chemistry is a health brand and the priority is to give people the right products to improve their health.
That's why tone of voice is also an important aspect of this brand. Bad copywriting can put off customers and make the brand lose all its credibility.

Always use proper syntax

Can we help send you new package?

Can we help you by sending a new package?

Use correct English

We ain’t got the rights to say that, honey.

We cannot disclose that information.

However, don’t phrase everything too formally

We sincerely apologize for any inconvenience this may have caused you, or will cause you in the near future.

We are sorry to hear that.

Use punctuation correctly

We are looking forward to hearing from you !!

We are looking forward to hearing from you!

Don't shout

Thank you for your order!!!!!!!!

THANK YOU FOR YOUR ORDER!

Thank you for your order!

Keep it professional

Omg we didn’t know that!! We will fix that!

Thank you for pointing that out! We appreciate your feedback.

Stay down-to-earth and honest

This fix-all-miracle will help you improve your life overnight!

Recent studies [1] have pointed out that this product can help people manage their pain more easily.*

* Always link to mentioned studies.

Communicate sincerely and caringly

We currently cannot find the solution, please contact us again in 3-5 businessdays for an update.

We are sorry to inform that we can’t offer a solution right now, we will contact you again in 3-5 businessdays with an update.

Closing/opening e-mails

Cheers, later, much love, ...

Kind regards, sincerely, ...

Don’t use playful language

The page ‘cbd edugfvcation’ isn’t here, maybe it ran away?!

The page ‘cbd edugfvcation’ does not exist, perhaps you were looking for ‘cbd education?’
