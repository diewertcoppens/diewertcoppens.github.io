General guidelines

These are some general guidelines to ensure consistency throughout all typographic uses.

For small format uses:

  • Titles are 2.5 times the size of the body text.
  • Paragraph spacing between the title and the body text is 1/2 the size of the title.
  • Paragraph spacing between paragraphs of body text is 1.5 times the size of the body text.
  • Leading is twice the font size for body text and subheads.

For large format uses:

  • Titles are 4.5 times the size of the body text.
  • Paragraph spacing between the title and the body text is 1/3 the size of the title.
  • Paragraph spacing between paragraphs of body text is 1.5 times the size of the body text.
  • Leading is twice the font size for body text and subheads.
